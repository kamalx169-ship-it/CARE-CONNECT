Health & Nutrition

Food is medicine -
we take it seriously

Proper nutrition underpins every child's development. Our care takers, guided by dietitian-approved protocols, ensure every meal supports each child's individual health and sensory needs.

  • ✓Dietitian-approved, allergy-conscious meal plans for every child
  • ✓Sensory food preferences respected - texture and taste-sensitive menus
  • ✓Hydration monitoring with hourly check-ins throughout the day
  • ✓Daily health vitals - temperature, mood, energy logged and reported
